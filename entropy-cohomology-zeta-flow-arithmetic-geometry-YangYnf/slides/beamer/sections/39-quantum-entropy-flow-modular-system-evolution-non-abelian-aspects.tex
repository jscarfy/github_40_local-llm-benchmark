\begin{frame}{Non-abelian symmetries in modular systems}
The modular transformation operator in non-abelian systems satisfies:
\[
[\zeta^{[k]}, \zeta^{[\ell]}] = \mathsf{Q}^{k,\ell},
\]
where $\mathsf{Q}^{k,\ell}$ represents the quantum correction term modified by non-abelian symmetries.
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}{Entropy flow evolution with non-abelian symmetries}
The entropy flow operator in non-abelian systems satisfies:
\[
\zeta^{[k]}(x) = x + \mathsf{Q}^{k}(x),
\]
where $\mathsf{Q}^{k}(x)$ reflects the quantum correction term influenced by non-abelian symmetries in modular transformations.
\end{frame}
