\begin{frame}{Advanced quantum corrections in modular systems}
The advanced modular system operator satisfies:
\[
[\zeta^{[k]}, \zeta^{[\ell]}] = \mathsf{Q}^{k,\ell},
\]
where $\mathsf{Q}^{k,\ell}$ represents the advanced quantum correction term.
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}{Entropy flow with quantum corrections}
The advanced entropy flow operator satisfies:
\[
\zeta^{[k]}(x) = x + \mathsf{Q}^{k}(x),
\]
where $\mathsf{Q}^{k}(x)$ represents the advanced quantum correction term affecting modular system behavior.
\end{frame}
